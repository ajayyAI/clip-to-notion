X to Notion Idea Saver

Connect Notion once, then save posts from x.com in one click.

  1. Create a Notion internal integration and copy its secret token.
  2. Create a database with the required properties listed below.
  3. Share that database with your integration in Notion.

Required Notion database properties
  • Title (title)
  • Post URL (url)
  • Author (rich_text)
  • Content (rich_text)
  • Posted At (date)
  • Saved At (date)
  • Source (select)
